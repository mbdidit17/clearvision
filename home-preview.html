FAQ

Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know before starting your coaching.

Do I need a gym?

Not necessarily. Plans are built around the equipment you have — full gym, home setup, or bodyweight.

Is this a subscription?

Yes — coaching is monthly so your plan keeps evolving as you progress. Cancel anytime.

Can beginners join?

Absolutely. Foundation is perfect if you're starting out and want your nutrition handled properly.

What's the difference between tiers?

Foundation is nutrition coaching. Enhanced adds a full personalised training programme on top.

How do we communicate?

Directly over WhatsApp — for check-ins, questions, adjustments and accountability.

How soon do I start?

Once you sign up and share your details, your plan is built and you're training within days.
